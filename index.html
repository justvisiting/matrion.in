03 / Engagement

How an engagement actually runs.

Gartner expects more than 40% of agentic AI projects to be cancelled by the end of 2027 — citing escalating costs, unclear business value and inadequate risk controls. Model capability is not on that list. We work the other way round: the measurement comes first, and a narrow slice reaches production before the roadmap widens.

Week 0

Scope and baseline

Pick one workflow with a real owner and a measurable outcome. Build the evaluation set before building the agent, so "better" is defined before anything ships.

Weeks 1–3

Thin slice to production

The narrowest useful version, running against real traffic behind approval gates — with tracing, cost controls and a rollback path from the first deploy.

Weeks 4–10

Harden and widen

Expand coverage as the evaluations hold. Failure handling, permissions, escalation paths and regression tests grow with the surface area, not after it.

Ongoing

Operate and hand over

Your engineers take ownership with the harness, the runbooks and the evaluation suite intact. We scale down as your team scales up.

04 / Governance

Built for the deadlines that are actually coming.

Agents that take real actions on real data land inside a compliance perimeter. We design for that from the first commit — traceable decisions, retained evidence, and permissions that survive an audit.

IN

DPDP Act — May 2027

India's substantive data protection obligations — notice, consent, safeguards, breach reporting, data principal rights — come into force 14 May 2027, with consent-manager provisions from November 2026. 2026 is the build-and-test year.

IN

Synthetic content labelling — already binding

The IT Amendment Rules 2026 have been in force since 20 February 2026, requiring synthetically generated information to carry visible or audible labels. If your agents produce customer-facing content, this applies today.

EU

EU AI Act — high-risk from December 2027

The Digital Omnibus moved standalone high-risk obligations from August 2026 to 2 December 2027, with transparency and marking duties from 2 December 2026. Prohibitions and AI literacy duties already apply. Relevant to anyone selling into Europe.

STD

ISO/IEC 42001 readiness

The emerging AI management system certification. We build the control evidence — evaluation records, model and data lineage, human oversight — as a by-product of delivery rather than a retrofit before an audit.
